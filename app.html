The Easyco App

For a greener future

"Sustainability focuses on meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs. The concept of sustainability is composed of three pillars: economic, environmental, and social-also known informally as profits, planet, and people."

The solution


Making a more sustainable choice has never been easier. Make your next purchase an eco-friendly one with Easyco!

Whether you need to buy some new shoes, a new fridge, or you just need to decide tonights dinner, Easyco has you covered. The Easyco app is a mobile application which aims at helping people make an eco-conscious purchase by sorting out the eco-friendly products that online and nearby stores have to offer.
