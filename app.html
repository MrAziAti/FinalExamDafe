The solution

"Sustainability focuses on meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs. The concept of sustainability is composed of three pillars: economic, environmental, and social-also known informally as profits, planet, and people."


- Mitchell Grant.


Our solution encompasses an app that will make it easier for people to choose an ecological and enviornmentally friendly option. This includes clothes, food, electronics and household products. By having an app that shows you all your available options it will take much less effort for people to make better decitions that overall helps our enviornment and society.

  • -Making a sustainable choice has never been easier. Make your next purchase an eco-friendly one with Easyco!


  • -Whether you need to buy some new shoes, a new fridge, or you just need to buy tonights dinner, Easyco has you covered.


  • -The Easyco app is a mobile application which aims at helping people make an eco-conscious purchase by sorting out the eco-friendly products that online and nearby stores have to offer.


  • -Search for this weeks discounted eco-within each category, or take a look at the offers page.


  • -Easyco uses 17 eco-labels that have been approved by Easyco and the norwegian consumer council in order to ensure that your purchase is a sustainable one.
