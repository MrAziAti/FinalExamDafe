When you go to your local supermarket today, wou will find a vast variaty of goods. Some of these will have som emblems on them, that be Svanemerket, Fairtrade or Debio. Todays everyday supermarket often contains a lot of different varieties of food. And that is a good thing, however many of the products that are sold are often harmful to the environment’s wellbeing. Blinded mass production has lead to many problems like climate change, massive deforestation, multiple species dying out and a process called ocean acidification.(source1)

In recent years many have come to the realization that if we don’t change our consumption that we eventually will end up destroying both animal and human conditions.
Therefor many have chosen to rather consume products that are more environmentally friendly and ethical like “svanemerket” or “GOTS”.
